Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2024

Summary: SPACE Terminal is designed with privacy in mind. Your conversations are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device (except when sent to AI providers for responses). We only collect what's necessary to provide the service, and we're transparent about exactly what that is.

Who We Are

SPACE Terminal is an experimental AI conversation platform that helps you think through complex problems with the help of AI advisors. The service is operated by SPACE Terminal and is available at spaceterminal.xyz.

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

SPACE Terminal stores all your data locally in your web browser using localStorage. This includes:

  • Complete conversation history and messages
  • Custom AI advisors you create
  • Saved prompts and preferences
  • Session settings (theme, sidebar state, etc.)

This data never leaves your device except when you send messages to AI services for responses (see "AI Service Providers" below). You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings or by using the in-app commands.

We do not store your conversations on any server or database. Your data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

When You Use the App

  • Nothing persistent on our servers. We don't create accounts or track you across sessions.
  • Your conversations are stored only in your browser's localStorage.
  • We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns (see below).

Third-Party Services

AI Service Providers

When you send a message in SPACE Terminal, your message is sent to one or more AI service providers to generate responses:

  • Anthropic (Claude) - Primary AI provider. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI (GPT) - Used for title generation and optional features. Privacy Policy
  • OpenRouter - Used for model selection and routing. Privacy Policy

These services process your messages to generate AI responses. They may temporarily store your messages according to their respective privacy policies. We use server-side API calls so your API credentials are never exposed to the browser.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use SPACE Terminal. This collects:

  • Page views and session duration
  • Device and browser information
  • Geographic location (country/region level)
  • Feature usage patterns

No conversation content is ever sent to Google Analytics. You can opt out using browser settings, ad blockers, or browser extensions like uBlock Origin.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the AI conversation experience
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve features

Data Security

  • All data transmitted to AI providers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Your conversation data stays in your browser's localStorage
  • We conduct regular security reviews

Your Rights and Controls

  • Data Export: Export your conversations in JSON or Markdown format using the in-app export feature
  • Deletion: Delete individual sessions, or clear all local data through your browser settings
  • Analytics Opt-out: Use browser settings or ad blockers to disable Google Analytics

Data Retention

  • Local storage: Retained until you clear your browser data
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained for product improvement

Children's Privacy

SPACE Terminal is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Open Source

SPACE Terminal is open source software. You can review our code, self-host your own instance, and verify our privacy practices at github.com/andrewblevins/space.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "last updated" date. Continued use of SPACE Terminal after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
